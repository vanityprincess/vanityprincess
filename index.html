WHAT IS GLUTATHIONE?



Glutathione helps with skin radiance and brightness by inhibiting the tyrosinase enzyme which converts our lighter melanin to the darker melanin. This is also the magical element of anti-aging and detox.



This element is naturally formed and synthesised in the liver from 3 amino acids – Cysteine, Glycine and Glutamic Acid. We have the highest levels of glutathione at the age of 20. Unfortunately these levels progressively diminish by 10% every decade.



Glutathione also helps with mental focus enhancement, addresses sense of brain fog, improves memory recall and assists with sleep quality improvement. Glutathione always go along with Vitamin C to enhance the antioxidant and detox benefits.

HOW CAN GLUTATHIONE LIGHTEN MY SKIN?


The colour in your skin occurs from the production of melanin and greater melanin production means darker skin. The antioxidants in Glutathione have been found to limit the production of melanin.

On top of this, they also contribute to speeding up cell turnover, replacing the damaged cells – that give a dark appearance to the skin – with new cells.
